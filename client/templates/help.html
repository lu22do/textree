Overview

This website allows to create and read text trees, which are texts organized in an inverted tree-like structure.

A text tree is made of branches, each branch being a piece of text connected to a parent branch and to several child branches. The starting point of a text tree is its the root branch.

Text trees can be read in a continuous mode allowing to switch branch while reading or in a branch by branch mode much like an adventure game where you don't see in advance the next branches until you choose one.

A text tree can be written in collaboration where various people contribute their branches to a text tree. It is also possible to keep the tree editable by a single person.

Your first tree

Here are the steps to build your first tree:

  • Go to My trees
  • Click on "Create a new tree"
  • Enter at least a name for your tree
  • Enter some initial text for the root branch and click on "Save edits"
  • To create child branches, click on the tree icon on the right of the text, then click on the "+" symbol
